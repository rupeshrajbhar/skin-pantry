We take pride in sourcing only the finest organic, ethically sourced, and vegan ingredients from around the world, handpicking them from places renowned for their exceptional quality. From Madagascan Vanilla Beans to Sri Lankan Coconut Pulp, all ingredients are carefully stored in ideal conditions to preserve their freshness and shelf life.

Our fragrant larder is overflowing with nature's finest ingredients, including rich Shea and Cocoa Butters, volcanic lava clays and pure essential oils. All of these ingredients are sourced locally and globally, and are raw, non-bleached, non-deodorised, plant-based, organically farmed, cruelty-free, and vegan, ensuring that our larder remains free of harmful chemicals and nasties.

We believe in the power of nature-based botanicals, phytonutrient-rich ingredients to create our formulations, and our passion for nature has driven us to develop a strong range of natural skincare that delivers visible results, keeping our customers coming back for more. Our ingredient-first approach reaffirms the way we think about natural and organically produced ingredients and their efficacy in skincare.
